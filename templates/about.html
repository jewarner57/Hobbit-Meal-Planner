About Us

We aim to provide an easy way to eat just like a hobbit from The Lord of The Rings' universe. You tell us how you eat and we suggest foods and provide recipes.

Our meal plan creator is powered by the spoonacular api. You can check it out here: Spoonacular

Our meals are designed using webstaurantstore's guide to hobbit meals.
